Web Development

Web development is the art of creating websites and web applications. It's a fantastic way for children to express their creativity while learning valuable technical skills. Through web development, kids can build their own digital spaces, share their ideas with the world, and learn the fundamentals of coding.

What You'll Need

  • A computer with internet access
  • A text editor (like Notepad, Visual Studio Code, or online editors like Replit)
  • A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
  • Basic reading and typing skills
  • Curiosity and creativity!
